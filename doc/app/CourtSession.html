    class CourtSession

    Validation

    court, date, start, and need must all be present.

    date

    must not be on a weekend.

    start

    must be an integer less than the seconds in 24 hours, and must be unique within the court and date.

    need

    must be less than PARALLEL_SESSIONS_MAX, defined in config/initializers/site_ruby.rb.

    Cascading

    It deletes dependent bookings and cancelled_bookings rather than destroying them. For bookings, this is because booking.destroy may create an infinite loop if this object has no reason_to_exist?. For cancelled_bookings, there is really nothing to destroy.
